The Village

Located close to the city of Corfu, Benitses appears as a small village, yet well-equipped to meet every visitor's needs. The village mainly develops along the road, offering comfort and tranquility. Among the tourist destinations of Corfu, this is one that retains the character of a traditional Greek village.

There are still some old houses and fishermen at the small harbor. You can also find the ruins of an old Roman villa and Roman baths.

The Beach
